## Sales Email Best Practices

### When to Send Sales Emails

**After Nurture:**
- 4-6 value emails ‚Üí 1 sales email (80/20 rule)

**Launch Sequences:**
- Pre-launch (3-5 emails) ‚Üí Launch (5-7 sales emails over 7 days)

**Promotions:**
- 1-2 sales emails during promotion period
- Always include end date

**Rule:** More value = more permission to sell

---

### Email Length for Sales

**Short (150-250 words):**
- E-commerce promotions
- Simple offers
- Flash sales
- Retargeting

**Medium (250-500 words):**
- Standard product launches
- Service offers
- Event registrations

**Long (500-800 words):**
- High-ticket offers ($500+)
- Complex services
- Detailed explanations needed

**Rule:** Higher price = longer email (need to overcome more objections)

---

### Essential Elements

Every sales email MUST have:

1. ‚úÖ **Clear offer:** What are you selling?
2. ‚úÖ **Price:** Don't make them guess
3. ‚úÖ **Value:** Why is it worth the price?
4. ‚úÖ **Social proof:** Who else bought/used it?
5. ‚úÖ **Risk reversal:** Guarantee, trial, refund
6. ‚úÖ **Urgency:** Why buy now vs. later?
7. ‚úÖ **CTA:** One primary button/link

**Missing any = lower conversion**

---

### Objection Handling

**Common objections to address:**

**"It's too expensive"**
‚Üí Show ROI, payment plans, compare to alternatives

**"I don't have time"**
‚Üí Show time savings, quick implementation

**"Will this work for me?"**
‚Üí Qualification criteria, testimonials from similar people

**"What if I don't like it?"**
‚Üí Guarantee, trial period, cancellation policy

**"I need to think about it"**
‚Üí Urgency (deadline), scarcity (limited spots)

**Address objections BEFORE they arise**

---

### CTA Best Practices

**Button text formulas:**

‚ùå **Generic:**
- "Buy Now"
- "Click Here"
- "Learn More"

‚úÖ **Specific & Benefit-Driven:**
- "Enroll Now & Save $500"
- "Get [Product Name] Today"
- "Yes, I Want [Outcome]"
- "Start My Free Trial"
- "Book My Strategy Call"

**Placement:**
- Primary CTA: Above the fold
- Secondary CTAs: Mid-email, bottom
- Repeat CTA 2-3 times (long emails)

---

### Urgency & Scarcity

**Genuine Urgency:**
‚úÖ Sale ends [specific date/time]  
‚úÖ Limited spots [actual number]  
‚úÖ Bonus expires [deadline]  
‚úÖ Early-bird pricing [tier pricing]

**Fake Urgency (Avoid):**
‚ùå Evergreen countdown timers  
‚ùå "Only 2 left!" (false)  
‚ùå Repeated "last chance" (but not really)

**Rule:** Only use urgency if it's REAL. Fake urgency destroys trust.

---

### A/B Testing for Sales Emails

**Test priority:**

1. **Subject line** (personalized vs. urgency vs. benefit)
2. **Email length** (short vs. long)
3. **CTA placement** (top vs. mid vs. bottom)
4. **Price presentation** (regular vs. strikethrough vs. payment plan)
5. **Social proof type** (testimonials vs. stats vs. case studies)

**Minimum sample:** 1,000 per variant  
**Test duration:** Full campaign period  
**Measure:** Click rate AND conversion rate (not just opens)

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Too many CTAs:** Confuses the buyer (one offer, one CTA)  
‚ùå **No urgency:** "I'll buy later" = never buys  
‚ùå **Hidden price:** Makes people suspicious  
‚ùå **No social proof:** "Am I the first buyer?"  
‚ùå **No risk reversal:** "What if it doesn't work?"  
‚ùå **Vague value:** "This will change your life" (how?)  
‚ùå **No segmentation:** Same pitch to everyone
