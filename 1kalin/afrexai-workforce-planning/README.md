# Workforce Planning Framework

Data-driven headcount planning, skills gap analysis, succession pipelines, and contingent workforce strategy — built for ops leaders and HR teams who need numbers, not theory.

Covers demand forecasting (3 models), gap analysis with priority scoring, supply strategies with cost benchmarks, succession planning, and quarterly review cadence.

**What's inside:**
- Revenue-per-employee and driver-based demand models
- Skills inventory and gap analysis with priority scoring
- Supply channel matrix (hire vs contract vs upskill vs automate)
- Succession planning framework with flight risk tracking
- Contingent workforce compliance (IRS, IR35, EU WTD)
- Full annual workforce plan template
- Quarterly review cadence
