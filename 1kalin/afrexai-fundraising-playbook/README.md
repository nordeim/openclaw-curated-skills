# Fundraising Playbook

Complete startup fundraising framework for Pre-Seed through Series C rounds.

## What It Does

Give your AI agent the context to help you plan and execute a fundraise — readiness scoring, round sizing with 2026 valuation benchmarks, investor targeting, pitch deck structure, term sheet analysis, and a 90-day execution calendar.

## Covers

- **8-dimension readiness assessment** with stage-specific benchmarks
- **2026 valuation ranges** by stage (Pre-Seed through Series C)
- **Investor targeting framework** — tiered list building with research criteria
- **Pitch deck structure** — 11 slides, what goes where
- **Term sheet red flags** — participating preferences, full ratchet, option pool traps
- **Outreach strategy** — 10-week sprint plan with urgency creation
- **Post-raise playbook** — first 90 days after closing
- **7 common fundraising mistakes** and how to avoid them

## Who It's For

Founders, CFOs, and operators preparing for or actively running a fundraise.
