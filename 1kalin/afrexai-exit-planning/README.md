# Exit Planning Framework

Business exit readiness assessment, valuation estimation, buyer targeting, and 24-month countdown checklist for founders planning an exit.

## What It Does

- Scores your exit readiness across 8 dimensions (0-100)
- Estimates valuation range using 2026 market multiples (SaaS, services, ecommerce)
- Maps buyer landscape (strategic, PE, individual)
- Generates 24-month pre-exit checklist
- Decodes deal structures (cash, earnout, rollover, asset vs stock)
- Tax optimization strategies (US + UK)
- Post-exit transition planning

## Who It's For

Founders, CEOs, and business owners preparing for acquisition, merger, or PE recapitalization. Works for SaaS, services, ecommerce, manufacturing, and professional services businesses.
