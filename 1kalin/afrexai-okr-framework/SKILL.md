# OKR Framework ‚Äî Objectives & Key Results Planner

Build quarterly OKRs that actually drive outcomes. Not vague aspirations ‚Äî measurable targets with scoring, alignment checks, and review cadences.

## What This Does

When triggered, walk the user through structured OKR planning:

1. **Company-level objectives** ‚Äî 3-5 per quarter max
2. **Team-level alignment** ‚Äî cascade from company, don't duplicate
3. **Individual OKRs** ‚Äî tied to team objectives, owned by one person
4. **Scoring & grading** ‚Äî 0.0-1.0 scale with calibration guidelines
5. **Review cadence** ‚Äî weekly check-ins, monthly scoring, quarterly retrospective

## How to Use

Say: "Help me set OKRs for Q2" or "Create OKRs for my engineering team" or "Review last quarter's OKR scores"

## OKR Writing Rules

### Objectives
- Start with a verb (Launch, Increase, Reduce, Build, Establish)
- Qualitative and inspiring ‚Äî the "what" and "why"
- Time-bound to the quarter
- Max 5 per level (company/team/individual)

### Key Results
- Quantitative and measurable ‚Äî the "how much"
- 2-4 per objective
- Must have a number: percentage, count, dollar amount, date
- Graded 0.0-1.0 where 0.7 = target (stretch goals should hit 0.6-0.7)

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 0.0-0.3 | Failed to make meaningful progress | Post-mortem required ‚Äî was it wrong goal or wrong execution? |
| 0.4-0.6 | Made progress but fell short | Identify blockers. Carry forward or adjust target? |
| 0.7 | Hit target | This is the sweet spot. Means goals were ambitious but achievable |
| 0.8-1.0 | Exceeded target | Either crushed it or goal wasn't ambitious enough. Calibrate next quarter |

## Alignment Check

For every team OKR, answer:
1. Which company objective does this support?
2. If this team hits all its KRs, does the company objective move?
3. Is any company objective orphaned (no team OKR supports it)?

## CFR Companion (Conversations, Feedback, Recognition)

OKRs work best with ongoing CFRs ‚Äî not annual reviews:
- **Weekly 1:1s**: 15 min, focus on blockers and priorities
- **Monthly feedback**: Specific, behavioral, forward-looking
- **Quarterly recognition**: Tied to KR achievement, public where appropriate
