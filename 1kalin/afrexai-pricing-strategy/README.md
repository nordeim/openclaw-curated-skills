# Pricing Strategy Analyzer

Stop guessing your prices. This skill turns your AI agent into a pricing consultant that analyzes your market, models tier structures, and finds the revenue you're leaving on the table.

**Fact:** A 1% price increase drives a 12.5% profit increase for the average business. Most companies underprice by 20-40%.

## What You Get
- Value-based pricing analysis
- Good/Better/Best tier modeling
- Competitor pricing matrix
- Willingness-to-pay estimation
- Revenue impact projections
- Discount policy recommendations

## Who It's For
- SaaS founders setting or adjusting pricing
- Agency owners packaging services
- Product managers launching new tiers
- Anyone who suspects they're leaving money on the table

## How to Use
Install the skill, then ask your agent:
- "Analyze my pricing for [product]"
- "Model a 3-tier pricing structure for [service]"
- "Compare my pricing against [competitor list]"
