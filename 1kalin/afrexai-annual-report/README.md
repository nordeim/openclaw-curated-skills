# Annual Report Generator

Generate board-ready annual business reports from your raw data. Financial performance, operational metrics, strategic review, and forward guidance — all in one structured output.

**Who it's for:** Founders, CFOs, Chiefs of Staff, finance teams preparing year-end reporting.

**What you get:**
- Financial summary with YoY comparisons and benchmarks
- Operational metrics (customer health, unit economics, team productivity)
- Strategic highlights and risk register
- Forward guidance with bear/base/bull scenarios
- Stage-appropriate benchmarks (Seed through Scale)

```
clawhub install afrexai-annual-report
```
