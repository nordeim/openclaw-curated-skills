# Employee Handbook Generator

Generate a complete, customized employee handbook in minutes instead of weeks.

Covers all standard sections: employment policies, compensation, leave, conduct, IT security, health & safety, and compliance. Adapts to your industry, jurisdiction, and work model.

## Quick Start

Install via ClawHub, then tell your agent:

> "Generate an employee handbook for [Company Name], a 45-person remote-first SaaS company in the US"

The agent walks through your specifics and produces a full handbook ready for legal review.

## What You Get

- Full handbook (11 sections, 30+ policies)
- One-page policy summary
- Employee acknowledgment form
- Revision log template

## Customization

Use flags to adjust: `--startup`, `--enterprise`, `--remote-first`, `--us`, `--uk`, `--eu`, `--tech`
