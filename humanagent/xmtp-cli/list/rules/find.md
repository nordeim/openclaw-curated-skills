## Find conversation

Find a conversation by Ethereum address or inbox ID.

```bash
xmtp list find --address 0x1234...
xmtp list find --inbox-id <inbox-id>
```

**Options:**

- `--address <address>` – Ethereum address
- `--inbox-id <id>` – Inbox ID

Use one of `--address` or `--inbox-id` to look up the corresponding conversation.
