# NDA Generator Skill

Generate professional Non-Disclosure Agreements for business meetings, partnerships, and employment.

## Quick Start

**Copy and paste the [SKILL.md](./SKILL.md) content into Claude, then describe your situation.**

## What It Does

- Creates one-way or mutual NDAs
- Customizes confidentiality terms
- Includes standard protective clauses
- Adapts for different jurisdictions
- Supports English and Chinese

## Example

**You**: I need an NDA for a meeting with potential investors where I'll share our business plan.

**Claude**: [Generates appropriate one-way NDA with investor-friendly terms]
