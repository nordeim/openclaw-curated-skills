# Cover Letter Template

## Variables
- `{{COMPANY}}` — Company name
- `{{ROLE}}` — Job title
- `{{HIRING_MANAGER}}` — Name if known, otherwise "Hiring Team"
- `{{COMPANY_HOOK}}` — Specific thing about company (product, mission, recent news)
- `{{RELEVANT_EXP}}` — Your matching experience
- `{{ACHIEVEMENT}}` — Quantified achievement
- `{{VALUE_PROP}}` — What you'll bring

---

Dear {{HIRING_MANAGER}},

I'm writing to express my interest in the {{ROLE}} position at {{COMPANY}}. {{COMPANY_HOOK}} — this is exactly the kind of challenge I want to tackle.

In my current role, I {{RELEVANT_EXP}}. A highlight: {{ACHIEVEMENT}}. I believe this experience directly translates to the challenges you're facing.

If given the opportunity, I'm confident I can {{VALUE_PROP}}. I'd welcome the chance to discuss how my background aligns with {{COMPANY}}'s needs.

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the possibility of contributing to your team.

Best regards,
Fikri Izzuddin

---

## Variations

### Startup (More Casual)
```
Hey {{HIRING_MANAGER}},

Saw the {{ROLE}} posting and got genuinely excited. {{COMPANY_HOOK}}

Quick pitch: {{RELEVANT_EXP}}. Did {{ACHIEVEMENT}}.

Would love to chat about how I can help {{COMPANY}} move faster.

Cheers,
Fikri
```

### Enterprise (More Formal)
```
Dear {{HIRING_MANAGER}},

I am writing to apply for the {{ROLE}} position at {{COMPANY}}, as advertised on [platform].

With [X] years of experience in [field], I have developed expertise in [skills]. At [Previous Company], I {{ACHIEVEMENT}}.

I am particularly drawn to {{COMPANY}} because {{COMPANY_HOOK}}. I am confident that my experience in {{RELEVANT_EXP}} would enable me to contribute effectively to your team.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my qualifications further. Thank you for your consideration.

Respectfully,
Muchammad Fikri Izzuddin
```

### Cold Outreach (Not Applying, Just Connecting)
```
Hi {{HIRING_MANAGER}},

Not applying for anything specific — just wanted to connect.

I've been following {{COMPANY}}'s work on {{COMPANY_HOOK}} and it resonates with what I'm building expertise in.

If you ever need someone with {{RELEVANT_EXP}}, I'd love to be on your radar.

Best,
Fikri
```
