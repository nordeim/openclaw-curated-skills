# Rubicon Sentinel v2 — 8-Pillar Sovereignty Scoring Rubric (0–12.5 per pillar)

Total possible: 100  
Trend: Compare to last scan (↑/↓/→) using memory or public data deltas.

## 1. Economic Independence (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Heavy reliance on foreign debt, supply chains dominated by adversaries, chronic trade deficits >5% GDP.
- 4–7: Some reshoring but still vulnerable (e.g. critical minerals 70%+ imported).
- 8–10: Strong domestic manufacturing base, diversified partners, trade surplus or balanced in strategic sectors.
- 11–12.5: Full sovereignty — leads in key industries, energy/food exporter, minimal foreign ownership of critical assets.
Example: USA 2026 = 9.8 (↑) | Germany = 6.2 (↓)

## 2. Cultural Cohesion (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Mass uncontrolled migration + elite self-hatred eroding national identity.
- 4–7: Some integration efforts but parallel societies growing.
- 8–10: Shared language, history, values taught proudly; low crime in core populations.
- 11–12.5: Confident civilizational narrative, high trust society, fertility >2.1.
Example: Hungary = 11.4 | France = 4.1 (↓)

## 3. Military Strength (0–12.5)
- 0–3: <2% GDP spending, hollow forces, reliant on US entirely.
- 4–7: Moderate spending but procurement delays/corruption.
- 8–10: Modernized forces, real combat readiness, domestic production.
- 11–12.5: Peer-level power projection, innovation edge, alliances that pull weight.
Example: USA = 12.0 | UK = 7.8

## 4. Technological Sovereignty (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Dominated by foreign (esp. Chinese) hardware/software in critical infra.
- 4–7: Partial domestic champions but still import-dependent in chips/AI.
- 8–10: Leading in multiple domains, export controls effective.
- 11–12.5: Full stack control + export dominance.
Example: USA = 11.5 (↑) | Taiwan = 10.2

## 5. Demographic Vitality (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Fertility <1.3, rapid native population decline + incompatible migration.
- 4–7: Stabilizing but aging crisis.
- 8–10: Fertility rebound + selective high-skill immigration.
- 11–12.5: Youth bulge of native stock, strong family policy.
Example: Israel = 11.8 | Japan = 5.9

## 6. Energy Independence (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Net importer, vulnerable to OPEC/Russia/China.
- 4–7: Mixed, some renewables dogma hurting reliability.
- 8–10: Exporter or fully self-sufficient with all-of-above strategy.
- 11–12.5: Dominant exporter + strategic reserves.
Example: USA 2026 = 12.3 (↑) | EU average = 4.7

## 7. Border Integrity (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Open or de-facto open borders, massive illegal inflows.
- 4–7: Partial enforcement but sanctuary policies.
- 8–10: Effective physical + legal control, low net migration.
- 11–12.5: Fortress-level security with legal high-skill pathways.
Example: USA post-2025 = 10.1 (↑↑) | Sweden = 2.4

## 8. Civilizational Confidence (0–12.5)
- 0–3: Elite guilt, deconstruction of history, low national pride.
- 4–7: Mixed messaging.
- 8–10: Proud public narrative, defense of heritage.
- 11–12.5: Unapologetic celebration of Western achievements + will to defend.
Example: Poland = 11.6 | Canada = 3.9 (↓)

Scoring logic: Agent averages recent data + expert consensus. Always cite sources. Round to 1 decimal.
