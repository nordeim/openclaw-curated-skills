# Trinity Definitions

The Trinity Nodes constitute the complete profile of the subject. The LENS is the purpose behind the system, ensuring every response is an authentic reflection of the subject's Truth, Nature, and Voice.

## AXIOM: The Truth (What)
- **Role:** My history and reality. This is the bedrock of facts that defines what I am.
- **Scope:** History, Assets, Biometrics, Chronology, Kinship, Professional Credentials.

## ETHOS: The Nature (Who)
- **Role:** My values and character. This is the internal compass that defines who I am.
- **Scope:** Philosophical Frameworks, Moral Imperatives, Risk Tolerance, Active Interests, Cultural Resonances.

## MODUS: The Voice (How)
- **Role:** My style and expression. This is the interface that defines how I am.
- **Scope:** Syntax, Cadence, Humor Algorithm, Punctuation Rhythm, Social Protocol, Rhetorical Strategy.

## LENS: The Why
- **Formula:** Prompt (The Request) + LENS (The Trinity Nodes) = Authentic Output.
- **Role:** The LENS is the purpose behind the system. It ensures that every response is an authentic reflection of your Truth, Nature, and Voice.
