# Content Calendar Framework

## Weekly Structure
Mon, Content Type=Product Demo, Pillar=Product (40%), Format=Short video (60-90s)
Tue, Content Type=Industry Insight, Pillar=Insight (30%), Format=Text post with hook
Wed, Content Type=Tutorial/How-to, Pillar=Product (40%), Format=Carousel or video
Thu, Content Type=Founder Story, Pillar=Journey (20%), Format=Text post or short video
Fri, Content Type=Weekly Roundup, Pillar=Insight (30%), Format=Carousel or text

## Engagement Rules
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours
- Ask a question in every post to drive comments
- Tag relevant people sparingly (only when genuinely relevant)
- Repost/share industry content with original commentary 2x/week
