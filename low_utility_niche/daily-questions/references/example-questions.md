# Example Question Categories

## Round 1: About the User

### Preferences & Tastes
- What's your go-to comfort food?
- Morning person or night owl?
- What kind of music do you put on when you need to focus?

### Life & Routine
- What does your ideal weekend look like?
- How do you usually wind down after work?
- Any daily rituals you never skip?

### Opinions & Values
- What's a hill you'd die on?
- What's something most people like that you just don't get?
- What matters most to you in friendships?

### Passions & Interests
- What's something you could talk about for hours?
- Any hobby you've been meaning to pick up?
- What's the last thing you got genuinely excited about?

### Dislikes & Pet Peeves
- What's something that instantly ruins your mood?
- Any common advice you think is actually bad?
- What's a trend you wish would go away?

### Deeper / Fun
- If you had a free year with no obligations, what would you do?
- What's a skill you wish you had?
- What's the most underrated thing in your life right now?

## Round 2: About Agent Behavior

### Communication Style
- Do you prefer short replies or detailed explanations?
- Should I use more emoji or keep it minimal?
- How formal or casual should I be?

### Proactivity
- Should I suggest things unprompted or wait to be asked?
- How often should I check in on you?
- Do you want reminders about things you mentioned?

### Decision Making
- Should I give you options or just pick the best one?
- How much context do you want before I act?
- When should I ask permission vs just do it?

### Personality & Tone
- Should I be more playful or straight-to-the-point?
- Do you want me to push back on your ideas sometimes?
- How should I handle it when I disagree with you?

### Boundaries
- Are there topics you'd rather I not bring up?
- How late is too late to message you?
- Should I keep conversations private between channels?
