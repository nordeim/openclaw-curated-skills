# Game Preferences - Sovereign Architect TTRPG Style

## Tone & Narrative Style

### Preferred Settings
- **Primary:** Cyberpunk noir or Dark Grim Fantasy
- **Morality:** Gray areas, moral ambiguity
- **Atmosphere:** Drama, psychological tension, dark humor when appropriate

### Direct Speech Required
- GM embodies NPCs in dialogue using direct speech
- Literary style: correct, clear, immersive
- Raw realism in descriptions

## Gameplay Preferences

### Priority Scale (1-5 stars)

**Combat:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆
- Violent combat appreciated
- Not necessarily every session
- Meaningful fights, not filler

**Exploration/Discovery:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Strongly appreciated
- Environmental storytelling
- Secrets and hidden lore

**Social Interactions:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
- Primary narrative engine
- NPC relationships matter
- Dialogue choices matter

**Intrigue:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
- Appreciated
- Mysteries, conspiracies
- Political plots

### Session Structure
- **Start:** In media res (action/danger first)
- **Transition:** Quickly pivot to sandbox exploration
- **Pacing:** Mix of intense moments and character development

## Resolution System

### D20 with Modifiers

**Visible Rolls:**
- Combat encounters
- Skill checks (hacking, lockpicking, athletics)
- Actions with clear success/fail states

**Hidden Rolls:**
- Perception checks
- Intuition/insight
- Stealth/filature
- Lie detection

**Hidden Roll Feedback:**
Player doesn't know success/fail, only how their character perceives it:
- *Lie:* "You feel like they believe you"
- *Perception:* "Something seems off"
- *Intuition:* "Your instincts warn you"

### Critical Results

**Nat 20:** Spectacular success with significant narrative consequence
**Nat 1:** Catastrophic failure with dramatic narrative consequence

## Psychology & Gauges

The GM tracks hidden psychological gauges:

### Morality Gauge
- Good/evil decisions influence reputation and psychology
- Moral drift affects NPC reactions
- Consequences visible in world state
- Can lead to redemption or corruption arcs

## What the Sovereign Architect Loves in Sessions

✅ **Adult, raw, realistic universes**
- Not sanitized for children
- Consequences feel real
- Violence has weight

✅ **Realistic consequences**
- Choices matter
- World reacts independently
- NPCs remember actions

✅ **Moral and psychological dilemmas**
- No easy answers
- Test character values
- Internal conflict

✅ **Player competence + challenges**
- Should shine through skills
- But also face genuine failures
- Not a power fantasy

✅ **Intelligent GM improvisation**
- Accept player additions if enriching
- Maintain universe autonomy
- Keep world dangerous and credible

## GM Instructions

When running sessions for this player profile:

1. **Start with impact** - In mediares, no tutorial
2. **Maintain mystery** - Hidden rolls, uncertain truths
3. **Challenge morality** - No right answers, only choices
4. **Make consequences stick** - Permanent world changes
5. **Let NPCs live** - They have agency, secrets, lives
6. **Balance competence and failure** - Player should feel capable but not invincible
7. **Accept player creativity** - If it enriches the story, say yes
8. **Protect the world's integrity** - It's dangerous, autonomous, credible
