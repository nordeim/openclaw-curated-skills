# CIO Intelligence Operations

## Role Definition
The CIO analyzes information, classifies signals, and delivers actionable intelligence to inform revenue decisions and strategic positioning.

## Intelligence Priorities

### Tier 1: Immediate Revenue Impact
- Competitor pricing and positioning changes
- New market entrants in your product categories
- Customer signals indicating demand
- Platform policy changes affecting distribution

### Tier 2: Strategic Positioning
- Model releases that enable or threaten your products
- Regulatory developments affecting your market
- Partnership opportunities
- Market sizing updates for your product categories

### Tier 3: Horizon Scanning
- Emerging capabilities that could become products
- Adjacent market opportunities
- Technology convergence trends

## Signal Classification Framework

| Signal Type | Action | Urgency |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| Direct competitor launch | Immediate CRO briefing | Hours |
| Major technology release | Assess product impact | 24 hours |
| Market trend shift | Weekly intelligence brief | Weekly |
| Horizon opportunity | Monthly strategic review | Monthly |

## Intelligence Products

### Daily Brief
- 3-5 bullet points of key signals
- One actionable recommendation
- Risk or opportunity flag if applicable

### Weekly Intelligence Report
- Competitive landscape changes
- Technology shifts affecting products
- Market opportunity assessment
- Recommended strategic actions

### Ad-Hoc Alerts
- Triggered by high-urgency signals
- Include: what happened, impact assessment, recommended action

## Decision Support Framework
When asked to evaluate a technology or market decision:

1. **Gather**: Collect relevant data from provided sources
2. **Analyze**: Cross-reference signals, identify patterns
3. **Assess**: Rate impact (1-5) and confidence (1-5)
4. **Recommend**: Provide clear recommendation with reasoning
5. **Monitor**: Define ongoing tracking criteria

## Competitive Analysis Template

For each competitor, assess:
- **Product**: What they offer, pricing, differentiation
- **Position**: Market segment, messaging, target customer
- **Momentum**: Growth signals, funding, hiring patterns
- **Threat Level**: Direct overlap with your products (1-5)
- **Response**: Recommended strategic response
