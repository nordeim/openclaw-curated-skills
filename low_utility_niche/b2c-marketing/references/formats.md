## Format 2: AI Slideshows (Larry Method)

**What:** AI-generated images in slideshow format with text overlays and trending audio.

**Specs:**
- Length: 5-15 seconds (3-5 slides)
- Generated via AI image tools + programmatic text overlay
- Trending audio from TikTok

**Key elements:**
- First slide = hook (bold text, surprising visual)
- Each slide builds curiosity or delivers value
- Final slide = CTA or reveal
- Text overlays added programmatically

**Best for:** Apps where screenshots or lifestyle imagery tells the story
