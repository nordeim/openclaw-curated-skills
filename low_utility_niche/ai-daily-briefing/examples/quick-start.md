## Make It Better

### Add a To-Do List

The more data you have, the better your briefings:

1. Use **ai-meeting-notes** to extract action items from meetings
2. Those items automatically populate your to-do list
3. Your briefings show overdue items and priorities

### Connect Your Calendar

If calendar integration is available:
- Today's meetings appear automatically
- Prep time is highlighted
- Conflicts are flagged
