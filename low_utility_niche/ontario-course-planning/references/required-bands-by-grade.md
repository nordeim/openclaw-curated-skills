# Typical compulsory bands by grade (editable)

> This is a checklist of the *typical* compulsory subject bands used when drafting a timetable. Exact compulsory-credit details depend on the school/board.

- Grade 9: English, Mathematics, Science, Core French, Geography, Physical Education, Arts
- Grade 10: Online learning opt-out (if applicable), English, Mathematics, Science, History, Civics/Careers
- Grade 11: Online learning opt-out (if applicable), English, Mathematics
- Grade 12: English
