## Comparison Summary

For 1,000 subscribers sending weekly newsletter:

| Tool | Best Plan | Monthly Cost | Notes |
|------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Mailchimp | Essentials | $13 | 5k email limit |
| Kit | Creator | $25 | Unlimited emails |
| Beehiiv | Launch | $0 | Free up to 2.5k subs |
| Buttondown | Basic | $9 | Best value for small lists |

**Recommendation by use case:**
- Just starting: Beehiiv Launch (free)
- Budget-conscious: Buttondown Basic ($9)
- Advanced automation: Kit Creator ($25)
- Legacy/established: Mailchimp Essentials ($13)
